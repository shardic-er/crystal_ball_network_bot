# Welcome to the Crystal Ball Network!

Greetings, adventurer! You have discovered the White Tower Banking Company's revolutionary Crystal Ball Network - the realm's premier magical marketplace.

## What is the Crystal Ball Network?

The CBN is a tesseract-enabled marketplace connecting buyers and sellers across the entire realm. Browse magical items from adventurers, craftspeople, estates, and collectors - then receive your purchases instantly through crystal ball delivery!

## About White Tower Banking

The White Tower began as a prestigious magic item shop before revolutionizing commerce by monopolizing crystal ball production. By networking these crystal balls together, they created the world's first magical telecommunications system - enabling international banking, credit, and now... instant magical shopping!

## Your Account & Balance

Every adventurer starts with **500 gold pieces** in their CBN account. The Curator of Bewildering Networks adjusts their demeanor based on your account standing:

- **500+ gp**: Professional and helpful service
- **100-499 gp**: Businesslike, mentions "affordable options"
- **10-99 gp**: Cool and curt
- **Below 10 gp**: The Curator becomes... less patient

Your balance and collection are tracked in your personal inventory thread, which is created automatically when you join the server.
